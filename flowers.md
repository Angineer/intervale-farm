Maureen raises a wide variety of cut flowers for sale at the farmer's market and the farm stand. In addition to these sales, we also offer flowers for special occasions such as weddings and anniversaries. Maureen does arrangements but also has flowers available by the bucket for customers who wish to do their own arranging. Please call or email for pricing and availability.
